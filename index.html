About Us

Our Mission

We, Green Planet Network, are a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting communities and the environment from the adverse impacts of global warming while promoting sustainable solutions.

What we do

We stand for clean air, water and land, healthy communities, and corporate accountability. We work for solutions that protect both the Earth’s resources and our communities.

Our Vision

We focus on the most significant threats to our most precious resources and fight to make sure that they are protected for future generations.

These next years are critical in the fight against climate crisis.
